The Observer is a deep-space exploration vessel assigned to investigate the Alpha Centauri system. Our mission is to collect scientific data, analyze potential habitable zones, and observe unknown Cosmic phenomena beyond charted space. All findings are considered Classified and vital to the future of interstellar travel.
